Preseed canvas

angel investors: Fill the canvas with the information and insights you get in the different stages of interaction with a startup team: receiving a pitch deck, discussing in calls with the team, doing market analysis and discussing with other investors. In the end, there's a consolidated view of all information (including a score) that ideally leads to more confident investment decisions.

Founders: Fill the canvas to challenge yourself on the different aspects of your business and make yourself more attractive to investors.

 

Usage: Click (or tap) to interact with all parts of the canvas. Single click to edit cards or texts, double-click (or long-click) on cell headings to get help, on a cell background to create a new card. Remove all text from a card to remove that card.

Storage: You can load and save the model to the browser's local storage (LS). Note that the data are not automatically deleted, so be careful about sensitive information. You can use Clear LS to remove everything from local storage. No information is stored on a server.

Design: This application is client-only, planning later addition of custom AI backends to pre-analyze a pitch deck, perform RAG background analysis and other use cases like generating investment briefs. It is a prototype to collect user feedback. Development was done using "junior coding" by an LLM, realizing there needs to be a clear set of requirements and a high-level design as the LLM does not know the bigger picture. This also reflects on how the pitch deck analyzer will be realistically used: as a "junior analyst" that prepares a decision.
