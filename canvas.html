Preseed canvas

angel investors: Fill the canvas with the information and insights you get in the different stages of interaction with a startup team: receiving a pitch deck, discussing in calls with the team, doing market analysis and discussing with other investors. In the end, there's a consolidated view of all information (including a score) that ideally leads to more confident investment decisions.

Founders: Fill the canvas to challenge yourself on the different aspects of your business and make yourself more attractive to investors.

Roadmap: Automated pitch deck and background analysis and report/deck generation.

 

Hints: You can single-click/tap (SC) or double-click/top (DC), alternatively long-click/tap (DC). SC to edit any element, DC to get help on cell headings. DC on cell to create a new card. Remove all text to remove card.

Note: This is a prototype, with the "junior coding" tasks done by an LLM. Insight: This version is client-only, with later additions of custom AI backends.
